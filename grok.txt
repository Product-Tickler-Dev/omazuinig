### 1. Executive Summary & Vision
**Oma Zuinig** is the friendly, sassy Dutch grandma who does the “kijken kijken” for you across **all** supermarkets (Albert Heijn, Jumbo, Plus, Dirk, Lidl, Aldi, etc.). She sweeps away “DUUR” prices, finds the cheapest basket, scans barcodes instantly, builds smart lists, and celebrates every euro saved with warm humor.

**Core Promise** (slogan on every screen):  
**“Kijken? Oma koopt slimmer!”**

**Market Opportunity (2026 data)**:  
Dutch grocery spend is price-obsessed. Consumers visit 2+ stores per week for deals, love “hamsteren” (bulk deals), and private-label growth is up. AH holds ~38% share, Jumbo ~20%, discounters (Aldi/Lidl) gaining fast. Existing tools (SmartKoop, Checkjebon, Btrkoop) are functional but cold. None have emotional branding or a mascot that makes saving *fun*. Oma Zuinig fills that gap: heritage + personality = viral retention + word-of-mouth.

**Success Vision**: 500k MAU in Year 1, €2M+ annual revenue by Year 3 via affiliates, premium “Oma Plus”, and merch. The app becomes the one Dutch households say “Ik ga even Oma Zuinig” about.

**Launch Strategy**: PWA-first on **omazuinig.nl** (installable home-screen app) → native iOS/Android wrappers. Zero download friction = instant reach.

### 2. Brand Identity & Heritage
**Heritage Tie-In**: Direct evolution of the iconic **Old Dutch Cleanser** mascot (1903–1970s). The “zuinige huisvrouw” with broom, apron, clogs, and bun symbolized cleanliness + thrift. Oma Zuinig updates this 100-year-old Dutch advertising archetype: she now sweeps *high prices* instead of dirt. Warm, self-mocking, never mean — pure Dutch DNA.

**Personality**:  
- Warm, no-nonsense oma (think every Dutch family’s favorite).  
- Dry humor: “Geen duur gedoe, hè!”  
- Empowering: thrift = superpower.  
- Always cheering you on.

**Voice**: Friendly Dutch directness + emojis. Short sentences. “Hoi lieverd!” “Slim gescand!”

### 4. Target Audience & Personas
**Primary**: 25-55yo Dutch households (families, young professionals, retirees) who hate wasting money but love the game of saving.  
**Secondary**: Expats learning Dutch thrift culture.

**Key Personas** (design/test against these):  
1. **Maaike (32, mom)** – busy, wants quick list + best price. Loves Oma’s “goede job, lieverd!”  
2. **Piet (68, retiree)** – classic “kijken kijken” pro, loves heritage feel.  
3. **Sophie (28, student)** – deal-hunter, shares screenshots.

### 5. Core Features & User Flows
**MVP (launch-ready)**:  
1. **Home/Dashboard** – “Hoi! Oma heeft vandaag €12,50 voor je bespaard!” + today’s top deals.  
2. **Scan & Vergelijk** – Camera scans barcode → instant multi-supermarket prices + Oma’s choice badge.  
3. **Mijn Lijst** – Smart shopping list with auto best-price per item + total savings.  
4. **Deals** – Curated weekly promotions + “hamster” alerts.  
5. **Mijn Oma** – Lifetime savings, thrifty level (Gouden Klompjes), stats, Oma’s personal message.

**Future (post-MVP)**: Family sharing, push “deal dropped” notifications, recipe integration, affiliate “direct bestellen” buttons.

**Data Sources** (backend): Real-time price scraping/APIs (legal partnerships or public feeds) for AH, Jumbo, Plus, etc. + user receipts.

### 7. Animations & Interactions (What Makes It Million-Dollar)
Mascot-driven, subtle, delightful (inspired by Duolingo 2026 tactics – 4.5x DAU lift). Use Flutter’s implicit animations + Rive/Lottie for complex ones.

- **Oma Idle**: Gentle bobbing + occasional wink.  
- **Deal Found**: Broom sweep animation clears “DUUR” red tags → green savings badge pops with confetti.  
- **Scan Success**: Barcode line animates, Oma appears with thumbs-up + speech bubble.  
- **Savings Total**: Numbers count up with sparkles.  
- **Loading**: Oma’s broom spins like a windmill.  
- **Error/Empty**: Oma shrugs with dry “Tja, soms is alles duur vandaag…”  
- **Micro-interactions**: Tap a deal → Oma cheers; add to list → item flies into basket.

All animations ≤300ms, buttery 60fps. No jank.

### 8. Technical Architecture (2026 Best-Practice)
**Core**: **Flutter 3.29+** (single codebase for PWA + iOS + Android).  
- **Why Flutter**: Proven for mascot-heavy UIs, PWAs, real-time, and 2026 performance (Impeller engine). One codebase = fast iteration.  
- **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL + Edge Functions + Auth + Realtime) or Firebase. GDPR-compliant, Dutch-friendly.  
- **Hosting**: Vercel (PWA) + Cloudflare.  
- **Key Packages**:  
  - camera + ml_kit or ZXing for scan.  
  - flutter_rive / lottie for animations.  
  - supabase_flutter for realtime prices/lists.  
  - url_launcher for “direct bestellen” affiliate links.  
- **PWA Config**: Installable, offline lists, push via Firebase Cloud Messaging (works on PWA).  
- **Security**: GDPR-first. Anonymous mode + full consent for price history.

**Deployment**: PWA on omazuinig.nl first (Week 1-8). Native apps via Flutter build (Month 2).
